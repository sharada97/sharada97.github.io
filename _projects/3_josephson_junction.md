Highlights:

- Patterned a three-layer Al (35 nm) / AlOx (1–2 nm) / Al (40 nm) stack using an AJA sputtering machine.
- Designed junction masks in K-Layout and used a Heidelberg MLA150 maskless aligner with S1800 photoresist.
- Performed AFM/SEM thickness measurements and refined sputtering and wet-etching parameters.
- Ran qubit simulations in Ansys HFSS and collaborated with SLAC to optimize dipole antenna structures minimizing radiative noise.
