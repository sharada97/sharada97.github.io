Using historical share-price data from 2000 to the present, this project applies a Markov Chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) simulation to predict the percentage change in a stock's price over the coming year, along with predicted-vs-actual growth statistics to assess accuracy.

- Building a Streamlit deployment for interactive forecasting.
- Code: [Predicting_Stock_Market_Using_MCMC](https://github.com/sharada97/Predicting_Stock_Market_Using_MCMC)
