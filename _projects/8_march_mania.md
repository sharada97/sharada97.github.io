A machine learning project for Kaggle's **March Machine Learning Mania** competition, predicting the outcomes of the NCAA college basketball tournament from historical team and game data.

- Engineered features from historical regular-season and tournament results to estimate matchup win probabilities.
- Evaluated with the competition's Brier-score metric on bracket predictions.
